Our Certificate & Online Programs For 2022

Accredited online certificate programs allow students to learn about particular subjects without pursuing a degree. Many colleges and universities offer certificate programs online.
Learners can earn certificates in fields such as education and technology. Schools also offer certificates in trades such as plumbing and welding.

Some certificate programs accept high school graduates, while others accept only college graduates. Keep in mind that certificates are not certifications for specific jobs. Certificates help prepare students for their desired careers.

This page highlights accredited online certificate programs. Read on for information on costs and program options.

About Us

Osmeña Colleges stands as the realization of the dream of its founder. Dr. Anacleto R. Peliño Señior..
to bring higher education within the reach of Masbateños. Who could not afford to send thier children to other places for a college education.
The school was founded in june 1948. holding classes in the rented building, at the corner of Mabini and Danao Street in the capital town of Masbate.
It was first known as Osmeña Colleges in honor of then President Sergio Osmeña. Sr. whose ideals, visions,
and aspiratios became the epitome of what the school stands today.
