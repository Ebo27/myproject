VISION & MISSION STATEMENT

Vision

Osmeña Colleges envisions graduates equipped with knowledge, skills and values. who will be productive and self-sufficient individuals.

Mission

With the aid of Devine Providence, Osmeña Colleges dedicates itself to develop a responsible citizenry who will be committed to serve God, country and home-


and to adhere and sustain the principles of productivity and self-sufficiency to translate development goals towards the attainment of a better quality of life.

About Us

Osmeña Colleges stands as the realization of the dream of its founder. Dr. Anacleto R. Peliño Señior..
to bring higher education within the reach of Masbateños. Who could not afford to send thier children to other places for a college education.
The school was founded in june 1948. holding classes in the rented building, at the corner of Mabini and Danao Street in the capital town of Masbate.
It was first known as Osmeña Colleges in honor of then President Sergio Osmeña. Sr. whose ideals, visions,
and aspiratios became the epitome of what the school stands today.
